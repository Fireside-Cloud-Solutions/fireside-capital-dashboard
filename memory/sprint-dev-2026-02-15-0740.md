## Work Completed

### 1. BUG-UI-NAV-001: Z-Index Conflict [P0 CRITICAL] ✅
**File:** `app/assets/css/responsive.css` (line 704)  
**Problem:** Hamburger menu used `z-index: var(--z-modal)` (400) instead of `var(--z-sticky)` (200)

**Impact:**
- Hamburger appeared ABOVE modals
- Broke modal focus trap (WCAG 2.1 violation)
- Users could click menu while modal was open

**Fix:** Changed to `z-index: var(--z-sticky)` (200)  
**Commit:** `79547c0`  
**Effort:** 2 minutes
