## Key Insights

### Major UX Problem
The Debts page tries to serve two audiences:
1. **Simple debt tracking** â€” People who just want to list credit cards/loans
2. **Advanced financing** â€” People who want amortization schedules and payoff plans

This creates confusion. Users don't know which section to use. Recommendation: Clarify OR merge.

### Feature Discovery Issue
The amortization modal is powerful (payment schedules, principal/interest breakdown, payoff date) but hidden behind a tiny text link. Most users never discover it.

### Empty State Gap
Both the Debts table AND the Financing cards have no empty states. New users see nothing and don't understand what these features do.
